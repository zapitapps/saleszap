Pricing FAQs

What happens when I hit my reply limit? ▼

When you hit your monthly limit, your bot sends customers a friendly upgrade message and your contact info. You get an instant notification to upgrade or wait for next month's reset.

Do limits reset monthly? ▼

Yes! Reply counts reset on the 1st of every month. Your paid plan period also resets 30 days from your payment date.

Can I change plans anytime? ▼

Yes. Upgrade anytime — takes effect immediately. Downgrade at your next billing cycle. No penalties or hidden fees.

Does Paystack charge extra fees? ▼

Paystack charges 1.5% + ₦100 per local transaction (capped at ₦2,000). This is paid by your customers on their orders. VendrAI does not take any commission on your sales.
