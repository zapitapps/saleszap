Frequently Asked Questions

Is VendrAI really free? ▼

Yes! The Free plan is 100% free forever — 100 AI replies per month, WhatsApp automation, orders, and Paystack payments. No credit card required to start. Paid plans start at ₦4,900/month for more replies and features.

Do I need coding experience? ▼

Absolutely not. VendrAI is built for non-technical founders. Our setup guide uses simple copy-paste steps. If you can send a WhatsApp message, you can run VendrAI.

Which languages does it support? ▼

VendrAI automatically detects and replies in English, Nigerian Pidgin, Yoruba, Igbo, and Hausa. More languages coming soon! Your customers don't need to type in English.

Is it WhatsApp-compliant? Will I get banned? ▼

VendrAI uses the official WhatsApp Cloud API (by Meta). This is the same API used by large businesses worldwide. Following our usage guidelines means zero risk of getting banned.

How does the self-healing AI work? ▼

VendrAI logs every AI response. From your dashboard, you see which answers worked best and can promote them to your Knowledge Base in 1 click. Future identical questions are answered instantly from KB — no AI call needed. It gets smarter automatically.

Can I sell digital products? ▼

Yes! Add your eBook, course, software license, or any download link. When a customer pays via Paystack, VendrAI automatically sends the download link via WhatsApp within seconds. Zero manual delivery.
