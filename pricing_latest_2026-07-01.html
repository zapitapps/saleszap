Pricing FAQs

Can customers pay without Paystack?

Yes. Tenants can add bank transfer, OPay, PalmPay, Kuda, Moniepoint and Cash on Delivery. Paystack is recommended for automatic confirmation, but it is not compulsory.

What happens if I exceed Freemium limits?

Your business can still receive messages, but automation limits apply. Upgrade to Starter, Growth or Pro for higher usage and more serious selling.

Can I upgrade anytime?

Yes. Choose your plan, enter your business ID and email, then complete payment securely through Paystack.
